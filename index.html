IV. Accessibility Commitments

This site was built by Brendan S for DH 101.

I have tried to make it accessible by:

  • Implemented visible keyboard focus states with :focus-visible outlines and interactive styling to make navigation easier.
  • Added alt text for artifact and image-related content to describe what is shown.
  • Included a low-motion sliding side navigation bar for quicker, less overwhelming movement through the page.
  • Used readable visual choices with larger line height, clear spacing, and high-contrast styling to improve usability.
  • Designed the website to work across different screen sizes and devices (while noting it may break after future updates).
  • Used accessibility tools and guidelines, including WCAG references, to review and improve the site.
  • WAVE Accessibility Evaluator reported an AIM score of 10 out of 10 for screen-reader presentation.
