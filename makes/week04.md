## Commentary
I first wanted to write an original storyline for this comic assignment and use AI to
experiment fine-tuning it. When I used AI for assistance, each response from AI seemed
incredibly generic. No ideas were proper to my style because AI lacked a nuanced perspective
and unavoidably used descriptions based on frequency in training data. For this reason, I decided
to use my own short story from an anthology I was a part of.
A multi-scene comic page for each panel was easiest for adapting my short story;
however, this brought up an issue with events flowing properly. In each panel, AI was restricted
to my section summary in the prompt, yet also came up with its own interpretation between
scenes. Even if I added more specific adjectives in my summary, AI relied on what was most
frequent in its training data about what these words meant.
From these two experiences, it became clear to me that AI constantly composed narrative
patterns, not nuanced stories. To tell an authentic story, regardless of whether it is a retelling, a
person has to properly understand the implications of events within the narrative. Based on my
personal experiences, using AI somewhat reminded me of having to work with modern writers
with whom I wrote this short story in response to.
