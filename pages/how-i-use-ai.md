# What was kept vs. changed?
Unless required by the assignment, most AI responses were changed to fit the specific use case. In most cases, a similar result could have been found by searching the internet.

- Most image prompts were slightly edited to continue a story (used in Make 3).
- For Make 8, the Suno "Write Full Song" feature was used with recycled prompts, adding quotes or related subject words tied to the Frankenstein novel.
- In both cases, some aspects of the AI's generation were kept, but the overall direction was set by the user.

# AI Use Policy
Let AI act as my legion while I, the general, create ideas and dictate direction.

In other words: ignore AI suggestions and edit additions when necessary until hitting a roadblock that requires feedback to proceed.
